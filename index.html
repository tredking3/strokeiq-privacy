Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

StrokeIQ ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use the StrokeIQ iOS app.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide when creating an account, including your display name, email address, and username. We also collect golf performance data including scores, heart rate data (with your permission), GPS location during rounds, and round statistics.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to provide and improve the StrokeIQ service, calculate your WHS Handicap Index, enable social features like leaderboards and activity feeds, and personalize your experience. We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

3. Health Data

Heart rate data is collected through Apple HealthKit with your explicit permission. This data is used solely to provide clutch moment detection and performance insights within the app. Heart rate data is stored locally on your device and optionally synced to your StrokeIQ account. We do not share health data with third parties.

4. Location Data

GPS location is used during active rounds to provide distance-to-pin measurements and auto hole switching. Location data is not stored permanently and is only processed during active rounds. We do not track your location outside of active rounds.

5. Data Sharing

Round statistics and leaderboard data are shared with other users according to your privacy settings (Public, Friends Only, or Private). You control what is visible to others. We do not share your data with advertisers or data brokers.

6. Data Retention

Your account data is retained until you delete your account. You may request deletion of your data at any time by using the Delete Account feature within the app under More then Account, or by contacting us at support@strokeiq.app.

7. Security

We use industry-standard encryption and security practices to protect your data. All data is transmitted over HTTPS and stored securely using Supabase infrastructure.

8. Children's Privacy

StrokeIQ is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes by updating the date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

10. Contact Us

For privacy questions or to request data deletion, contact us at:
privacy@strokeiq.app
support@strokeiq.app
